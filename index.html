About IBFO

The International Business & Finance Olympiad (IBFO) is an academic challenge designed to inspire the next generation of entrepreneurs, analysts and policy thinkers. The Winter Challenge edition features accessible online qualifiers and a selective final.

  • Language: English
  • Format: Online qualifiers → Global Finals
  • Target: Secondary & pre‑university students (Grades 8–12 / Year 9–13)

FAQ

Who can participate?

Students in secondary or pre‑university education worldwide. No previous olympiad experience required.

What equipment do I need?

A computer with stable internet, webcam for proctoring (finals), and a modern browser.

What is the syllabus?

Fundamental concepts in business, finance, and economics, with emphasis on applied reasoning and data.

Can schools register groups?

Yes. Teachers can submit a roster and receive a consolidated report.
