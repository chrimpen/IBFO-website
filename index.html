About IBFO

The International Business & Finance Olympiad (IBFO) is an academic challenge designed to inspire the next generation of entrepreneurs, analysts, and policy thinkers. Beyond the spirit of competition, IBFO serves as a unique platform for students to network, collaborate, and exchange ideas. It unites bright young minds from around the world, offering opportunities to learn from one another, build lasting friendships, and connect with likeminded peers who share a passion for business and finance.
